## The Hive Community

The Hive is more than a makerspace — it's a **community of makers, engineers, artists, and innovators**. We host events throughout the semester designed to bring people together around shared interests and collaborative projects.

---

## Workshops & Skill Sessions

We run regular hands-on workshops open to all GT community members. Workshops are free and cover topics like:

- **Intro to 3D Printing** — learn FDM basics, slicing software, and design for print
- **Laser Cutting Fundamentals** — from vector files to finished parts in one session
- **PCB Design & Soldering** — design your first printed circuit board
- **Vinyl Cutting & HTV** — make stickers, decals, and custom apparel
- **E-Textiles** — combine fabric with electronics for wearable projects

Workshop schedules are published each semester and RSVPs go out via the **weekly newsletter** — [subscribe here](https://hive.ece.gatech.edu/mailing-list/)

## Student Organizations

Many GT student organizations partner with the Hive for fabrication space and resources. If your club or team needs a place to build, [reach out to us](/contact) about collaboration.

## Become a Peer Instructor (PI)

Are you interested in being part of the team that keeps The Hive running? **Become a PI (Peer Instructor)** — a student volunteer who staffs the space, helps other makers, and builds expertise across our equipment areas.
