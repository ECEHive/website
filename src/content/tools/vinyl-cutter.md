## Machines Available

- **Silhouette Cameo 3**
- **Silhouette Cameo 4**
- **Silhouette Cameo 5**

All three machines use the computer located next to them. The design application is **Silhouette Studio**, which works similarly to Adobe Illustrator.

## Materials

The Hive stocks vinyl, cardstock, and stencil material. We have a variety of vinyl types available for cutting.

## Common Uses

- Custom stickers and decals
- Heat-transfer vinyl (HTV) for t-shirts and apparel
- Stencils for painting or etching
- Paper and cardstock cutting
- Product labeling
