The crafts area is located by the benchtop desks, near the staircase to the lower floor.

## Sewing & Embroidery

Sewing and embroidery machines are available. An updated embroidery machine (Melco EMT16X) is being set up — check with a PI for current availability.

## Dye Sublimation

Transfer vibrant, durable designs onto fabric and hard substrates:

- **Sawgrass SG 500** sublimation printer
- **Stahl's Hotronix** thermal transfer heat press

## Button Press

- **VEVOR Button Maker Machine** — 1" (25mm) badge/pin maker

## Project Ideas

- Custom t-shirts, tote bags, and apparel via sublimation
- Wearable electronics and e-textiles
- Custom pins and badges
- Bags, totes, and costume work
