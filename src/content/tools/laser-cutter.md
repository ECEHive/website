The Hive has three laser cutters located in the production studio on the first floor. You can see them through the windows at the IDC's ground entrance.

## CO₂ Laser Cutters (×2)

Made by **Universal Laser Systems**, these are our most popular tools.

- **Bed size:** 32" × 18"
- **Supported materials:** wood, acrylic, leather, cardboard, paper, fabric, and some metals (engraving only)
- **Rotary attachment** available for curved surfaces
- Spots are first-come, first-served (afternoons/evenings tend to be busiest)

## Fablight 4500 Fiber Laser

A non-continuous fiber laser capable of cutting through **¼" steel** quickly and cleanly. It replaced the plasma cutter.

- **Best for:** mild steel, stainless steel, anodized/bare aluminum, brass, copper
- Also great for engraving metals

## File Formats

- **Vector** (`.svg`, `.dxf`, `.ai`) — for cutting and vector engraving
- **Raster** (`.jpg`, `.png`) — for raster engraving

## Training

Laser training is required before your first use. Ask a PI to show you the ropes.
