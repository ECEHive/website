Resin printers produce extremely fine detail that FDM printers can't match. Ideal for intricate designs where surface finish matters.

## Supported Resins

- Standard resin (grey, clear, white, black)
- Water-washable resin

## Requirements

- Mandatory safety orientation before first use
- Gloves and eye protection required at all times
- Parts must be washed and post-cured in the provided station
