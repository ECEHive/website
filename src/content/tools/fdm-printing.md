The Hive offers a fleet of FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) 3D printers available to all Georgia Tech students, faculty, and staff. These printers are great for quick prototypes and functional parts.

## Available Materials

- **PLA** — easiest to print, good for most applications
- **PETG** — better heat resistance and durability
- **TPU** — flexible, rubber-like parts

## Tips for Success

- Bring your `.stl` or `.3mf` file ready to slice
- File at the front desk to request a print slot
- Check the Live Dashboard for current printer availability
