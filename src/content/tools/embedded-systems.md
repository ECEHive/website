The Hive has a large selection of electronics available for checkout at the front desk. Items are allowed to be used while in the space, and must be returned before leaving.

## Available for Checkout

- **Arduino** microcontrollers (various models)
- **Raspberry Pi** single-board computers
- **Texas Instruments LaunchPads**
- **Adafruit LilyPad** and other Adafruit boards
- **BeagleBone** boards
- Raspberry Pi accessories, breakout boards, USB cables, motors, and more

Exchange your BuzzCard at the front desk to check out items. Items are available for use **within the space only** — we no longer check out inventory to take outside the space.

## Choosing a Microcontroller

There is no single "best" microcontroller — the right choice depends on your project. Consider:

- **Processing speed vs. power consumption**
- **Number of I/O pins**
- **Connectivity needs** (WiFi, Bluetooth, Ethernet)
- **Real-time requirements**

Check out the [embedded systems selection guide](/guides/embed-selection/) on the Hive Wiki for help choosing the right board for your project.
