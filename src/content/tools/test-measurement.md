Each standard benchtop station includes an oscilloscope, function generator, multimeter, and power supply. In addition, the Hive has a suite of more advanced instruments available.

## Standard Station Equipment

- **Oscilloscope** — Tektronix MSO58 or Keysight DSOX3014A / DSOX3034A / MXR804A
- **Function generator** — Tektronix AFG31152 or Keysight 33500B waveform generator
- **Digital multimeter** — Keithley DMM6500 or Keysight 34460A
- **Power supply** — Keithley 2230-30-1 triple output or Keysight U8031A (2×30V/6A + 5V/3A)

## Advanced / Specialized Instruments

- **Keithley 2450 SourceMeter** — precision voltage/current source and measurement
- **Keithley 2281S** — precision supply and battery simulator
- **Keysight E4980-AL LCR meter** — component characterization (L, C, R, ESR)
- **Keysight N1913A power meter** — RF power measurement
- **Keysight network analyzer** — S-parameter measurements
- **Keysight signal analyzer** — spectrum/signal analysis
- **Keysight vector signal generator**

Beginner guides are available for the oscilloscope, function generator, and multimeter on the Hive Wiki. Video tutorials are also available on our YouTube channel.
