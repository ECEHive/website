For **individuals and small groups**, walk-in tours are available anytime during open hours — just come in and ask a PI!

For **large groups and organizations**, email us at [hive@ece.gatech.edu](mailto:hive@ece.gatech.edu) with:

- Point of contact
- Tour group size
- Suggested dates
