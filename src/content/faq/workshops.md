question: "How do I attend a workshop?"
category: "Workshops"
order: 4
---

Workshop RSVPs are sent in the **weekly newsletter** every Monday morning. Subscribe via the link at the bottom of this page. Once you receive the newsletter, click the RSVP link for the workshop you want — spots fill quickly!

Workshops are free unless materials are needed (e.g., a soldering kit). Check the calendar for upcoming events.
